blop (position_or_object, itemstring_or_stack, x_strength, y_strength, z_strength)
"Blops" the item into existence at the given position and assigns it a random velocity/acceleration.

Parameters:

  • position_or_object The position, a pos value or an ObjectRef.
  • itemstring_or_stack The item string or an ItemStack.
  • x_strength Optional. The strength of the random movement in the x direction, defaults to 5.
  • y_strength Optional. The strength o the random movement in the y direction, defaults to 5, minimum is 1.
  • z_strength Optional. The strength of the random movement in the z direction, defaults to 5.

Returns:

    The spawned item.
